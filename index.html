Hi! I'm Tuyal Shrestha, a Computer Engineering graduate with a deep passion for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I love turning complex problems into elegant, working solutions — whether that's building a language model from scratch, training a vision system, or crafting clean web interfaces.

My journey into ML started with curiosity about how machines understand language. That curiosity has since grown into hands-on projects spanning LSTM-based text prediction, image captioning with CNN+LSTM architectures, and fine-tuning multilingual transformers for low-resource languages.

When I'm not training models or debugging gradients, I enjoy exploring new research papers, contributing to open-source projects, and occasionally hiking in the mountains near Kathmandu.
